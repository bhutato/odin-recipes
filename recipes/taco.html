Taco

Ingredients

  • ½ kg lean ground beef
  • ½ teaspoon onion powder
  • ½ teaspoon garlic salt
  • ½ teaspoon celery salt
  • ½ teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 can tomato sauce, or more to taste

Steps

  1. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Cook and stir ground beef in the hot skillet until browned and crumbly, 5 to 7 minutes.
  3. Season beef with onion powder, garlic salt, celery salt, and cumin.
  4. Pour tomato sauce over beef, stir to coat, and simmer until slightly thickened, about 5 minutes
